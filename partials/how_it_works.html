How Hamlet Works

Hamlet is an online marketplace that caters to both specialists and people looking to learn or enhance a skill or hobby. Our platform allows “skill seekers” to find and connect with people in their own community who posses those skill and are eager to teach others.

For Specialists

  • Only $7 per month to post your profile
  • Free one month trial
  • Advertise special skills and upload photos of your work
  • Set your own hourly rates*

For Skill-Seekers

  • Free and unlimited access to specialist profiles
  • Category and keyword searches for specific skills
  • Customer reviews and ratings of specialists
  • Learning face-to-face from experienced individuals

* HAMLET SIMPLY CONNECTS INDIVIDUALS; RATES AND COORDINATING MEETINGS ARE UP TO YOU
